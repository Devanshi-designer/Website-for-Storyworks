Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is Label purely cloud-based or can it run on-premise?

A: Label is cloud-native SaaS, but for large enterprise clients with strict security mandates, we offer a dedicated private cloud or hybrid on-premise deployment solution. Contact our business development team for a custom assessment.

Q: What specific file analysis methods are used for media types?

A: For visual media, we employ Object Recognition (CNNs). For audio, we use Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR). For documents, we combine OCR with Natural Language Processing (NLP) for keyword and context extraction.

Q: How is data security and ownership handled?

A: Your data remains yours. All transfers and data at rest are protected with AES-256 encryption and TLS 1.3. We operate a strict zero-retention policy; client assets are permanently purged from our processing servers immediately upon final deployment approval.
