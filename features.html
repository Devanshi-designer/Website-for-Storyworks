Features

A concise breakdown of what LABEL can do for your pipeline.

Video: Scene & Object Detection

Automatic scene boundary detection, per-scene thumbnails, and object tags to include in your naming template.

Audio: Transcription & Speaker ID

Fine-grained speech-to-text with speaker diarization so names can include speaker labels or quotes.

Images: Classification & OCR

Detect faces, landmarks, logos, and extract text from images (OCR) for meaningful filenames.

Templates & Mapping

Define client naming templates (JSON/YAML) with placeholders for date, project, scene, speaker and more.

Verification UI

Human-in-the-loop review before final export — approve, edit, or reject suggested names.

Audit & CSV Reporting

CSV mapping and JSON logs for downstream automation and auditing.
